Your First Visit

New to this? Here's exactly how it goes.

Most people who book have never had a session before. There's nothing to prepare and nothing you need to know in advance — here's what the hour actually looks like.

01

Before you arrive

Wear something comfortable. Come as you are — no preparation needed. Every session is one-on-one in a private, candlelit room.

02

We talk first

We start with a short, easy conversation about what brought you in and what you'd like to focus on. You set the pace; nothing is required of you.

03

The session

You rest, fully clothed, on a comfortable table. Reiki is gentle, light-touch or no-touch energy work. Many people feel warmth or deep calm — some simply relax. There's no wrong way to experience it.

04

Afterward

We talk through anything that came up, and Nicole shares simple ways to carry the calm home with you. Most people leave feeling lighter and more grounded.

A grounded note: Reiki, sound healing, and the sessions here support relaxation, stress relief, and a calmer nervous system. They're a complement to — not a replacement for — medical or mental-health care.
